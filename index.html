Elves are often depicted as beings of immense grace, longevity, and a deep connection to the natural or magical world. Their names often reflect these qualities, carrying sonorous tones and meanings tied to elements, celestial bodies, or virtues. Whether you seek a name that speaks of ancient power, sylvan grace, or starlit mystery, the essence of elven culture provides a vast wellspring of inspiration.
